About Hare Rama Kshetram

The orphan ashram is established and organized by the Hare Rama Kshetram Public Charitable Trust registered as a non-profitable and service oriented organization.

On observing the miserable conditions of several senior citizens and street children, Sri P. Eswar Reddy, Retired Superintendent, Z.P Office, Chittoor; had decided to help such needy aged people and children. Immediately after his retirement he has initiated and established this Hare Rama Kshetram public charitable trust.

The main aim of the trust is to provide free care and support to the elderly people and orphans.
